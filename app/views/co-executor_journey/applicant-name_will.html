We need to know if your name is written differently in the will or any codicils.

This could be because:

  • you changed your name after the will was made
  • part of your name was not included in the will
  • there was a spelling mistake in the will
  • you’re also known by a different name

If there is a difference, you’ll be able to tell us why later.
