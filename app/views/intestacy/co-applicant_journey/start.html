Apply for probate

Max Mustermann has started an application for a grant of probate to deal with Erika Mustermann’s estate, and has named you as an applicant.

All applicants named on the grant will be given the legal right to:

  • collect the estate’s assets (property, money and possessions)
  • pay any taxes and debts
  • distribute what is left to the people entitled to it

To continue, you will need to:

  • check if the information Max Mustermann has given is correct
  • confirm you agree with the legal statement

You will need Erika Mustermann’s date of death to access the legal statement.

If you do not want to apply for probate

You can choose not to apply if you do not want to manage the estate. This does not affect your right to inherit.

You can either:

  • let Max Mustermann apply without you
  • appoint someone else to apply on your behalf – you will need to complete Form PA12 (opens in a new tab)

You will need to contact Max Mustermann and ask them to change the information they have given if you do not want to apply.
