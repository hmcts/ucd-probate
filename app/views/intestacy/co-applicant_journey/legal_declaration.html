Legal statement

We, Max Mustermann, of 3 High Street, London SW11 6AA, and EXAMPLE NAME, of 76 Main Avenue, London, SW13 2LH, make the following statement:

That Erika Mustermann, died on 29 November 2024 intestate.

Declaration

We confirm that we will administer the estate of Erika Mustermann according to law. We will:

  • collect the whole estate

  • provide the full details of the estate and how it has been administered
