You have disagreed with the legal statement

You now need to contact Max Mustermann to discuss changing the information they have given.

As soon as Max Mustermann updates the information, they should contact you. You will then be able to access the updated legal statement using the same link we emailed you.

If you cannot reach an agreement

Probate staff cannot help with any disagreements between applicants. You will need to find another way to reach an agreement, which may include getting legal advice (opens in a new tab).

Max Mustermann will not be able to submit the application until all applicants have agreed with the legal statement and made their declaration.
