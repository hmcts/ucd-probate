Up to 4 people, including yourself, can apply for probate.

All applicants will be given the legal right to deal with {{ data['deceased-firstName'] }} {{ data['deceased-lastName'] }}'s estate. This includes:

  • collecting the estate’s assets (property, money and possessions)
  • paying any taxes and debts
  • distributing what is left to the people entitled to it

Not applying does not affect a person’s entitlement to inherit.
