The name you give us will be written on the grant of probate.

Your name should match exactly what is written on your passport or other official identification document, such as a driving licence.

You should make sure that this is the same form of identification you use when you are dealing with the estate of the person who died.

! Warning If the name on the grant does not match the identification you use, you may have difficulties dealing with the estate.
