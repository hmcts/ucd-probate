Declaration

We confirm that we will administer the estate of {{ data['deceased-firstName'] }} {{ data['deceased-lastName'] }} according to law. We will:

  • collect the whole estate
  • keep full details (an inventory) of the estate
  • keep a full account of how the estate has been administered

If the probate registry (court) asks us to do so, we will:

  • provide the full details of the estate and how it has been administered
  • return the letters of administration to the court

We understand that:

  • our application will be rejected if we do not answer any questions about the information we have given
  • proceedings for contempt of court may be brought against the undersigned if it is found that the evidence provided is deliberately untruthful or dishonest, as well as revocation of the grant
