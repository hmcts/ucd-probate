Check if you can apply online

You cannot apply for probate online

As your relationship to the person who died is not listed, you cannot use the online service to apply for probate.

You may still be able to apply for probate by post. Check if you are the most entitled person to apply (opens in a new tab) before you start.

If you are the most entitled person, you can use Form PA1A (opens in a new tab) to apply.
